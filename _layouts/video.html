XCMG brand introduction

Xuzhou Construction Machinery Group Co., Ltd. was established in March 1989. In April 1997, it was approved by the State Council as one of the 120 pilot enterprise groups in the country. It is one of the 520 key national enterprises and a national 863/CIMS application demonstration pilot enterprise. It is China's largest construction machinery development, manufacturing and exporting enterprise. XCMG Group adheres to the enterprise spirit of "strictness, steadfastness, progress and innovation", and in accordance with the diversified development strategy centered on construction machinery, has established a technology development system centered on a national-level technology center, and continuously develops high value-added, high-quality products.
